Peanut Butter Cream Cold Brew

Description

I recently came up with a copycat pumpkin cream cold brew. I liked it so much I tried making it with a different flavor. This worked out deliciously.

Ingredeints

  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream
  • 3 tablespoons sugar-free peanut butter-flavored syrup (such as Torani®)
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon powdered peanut butter (such as PB2®)
  • ice
  • 2 tablespoons sugar-free vanilla syrup (such as Torani®)
  • 1 cup cold brew coffee, or as needed

Direction for the Ingredeints

  1. Combine heavy whipping cream, syrup, peanut butter, and powdered peanut butter in a bowl.
  2. Whip with a hand mixer until creamy and slightly thickened.
  3. Fill 2 glasses with ice. Add 1 tablespoon vanilla syrup to each glass
  4. Fill 2/3 of the way with cold brew coffee, and divide peanut butter cream evenly between both.

Calories per serving

Per Serving: 306 calories; protein 5.8g; carbohydrates 5.3g; fat 30.3g; cholesterol 81.5mg; sodium 111.7mg
